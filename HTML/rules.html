Game Rules

Level 1

This level is deemed as the Addition level. The intention of this level is to test the player’s ability to add basic numbers.
Easy mode: 5 balloons, 120 seconds, 10 attemps and slow speed.
Normal mode: 9 balloons, 90 seconds, 10 attemps and normal speed.
Hard mode: 20 balloons, 50 seconds, 10 attemps and fast speed.
Extreme mode: 50 balloons, 50 seconds, 10 attemps and very fast speed.

Level 2

This level is deemed as the Subtraction level. The intention of this level is to test the player’s ability to subtract basic numbers.
Easy mode: 5 balloons, 150 seconds, 10 attemps and slow speed.
Normal mode: 9 balloons, 120 seconds, 10 attemps and normal speed.
Hard mode: 20 balloons, 70 seconds, 10 attemps and fast speed.
Extreme mode: 50 balloons, 70 seconds, 10 attemps and very fast speed.

Level 3

This level is deemed as the Division level. The intention of this level is to test the player’s ability to divide basic numbers.
Easy mode: 5 balloons, 150 seconds, 10 attemps and slow speed.
Normal mode: 9 balloons, 120 seconds, 10 attemps and normal speed.
Hard mode: 20 balloons, 70 seconds, 10 attemps and fast speed.
Extreme mode: 50 balloons, 80 seconds, 10 attemps and very fast speed.
